Privacy Policy
Information We Collect

JobScout is a job search aggregation tool that helps you find opportunities across multiple job portals:

  • We collect search queries to provide relevant job results
  • Basic usage analytics to improve the search experience
  • No personal information is stored permanently
  • No user accounts or registration required
How We Use Your Information

The information is used solely to:

  • Provide relevant job search results from multiple portals
  • Improve search algorithms and user experience
  • Monitor system performance and reliability
Data Security

We implement basic security measures to protect your search queries. Since no personal data is stored, your privacy is maintained by design.

Third-Party Services

JobScout aggregates publicly available job listings from job portals like Indeed, TimesJobs, and Naukri. We do not share any information with these services.

Contact

For any questions about privacy or the service, you can reach out through the contact information provided on the website.
