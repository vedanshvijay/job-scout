About JobScout

A powerful job search platform that aggregates opportunities from India's leading job portals to help you find your dream job faster.

JobScout simplifies your job search by bringing together listings from multiple sources, applying intelligent filtering, and presenting you with the most relevant opportunities in one convenient location.

Start Searching View Tech Stack

Our Mission

To revolutionize job searching in India by providing a unified platform that saves time, increases efficiency, and connects job seekers with their ideal opportunities across multiple job portals.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted job search companion, empowering millions of job seekers with intelligent search capabilities and comprehensive job market insights.

Key Features

Smart Search

Search across multiple portals with a single query. Get comprehensive results from Indeed, TimesJobs, and Naukri.

Real-time Results

Get fresh job listings as they are posted. Never miss out on new opportunities.

Advanced Filtering

Filter results by relevance, experience, salary, and more to find the perfect match.
